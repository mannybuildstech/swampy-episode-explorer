🌿 Family Adventure Starter

Turn Screen Time into Green Time

Plan a simple weekend roadtrip where each stop gets kids moving, noticing nature, and making memories together.

Start the roadtrip plan

Roadtrip Blueprint

  1. Pick your park or trail.
  2. Build hype with a story before you go.
  3. Turn the walk into a discovery mission.
  4. Close the day with birds, stars, and wonder.

Your Roadtrip Stops

Tap any stop to open the app and bring the activity to life.

  • Stop 01 · Route Planning

    AllTrails

    Pick a nearby trail or park that fits your family — from easy walks to full adventures.

    Nature connection: Helps families choose a place and get outside faster.

    Open AllTrails

  • Stop 02 · Pre-Trip Story

    Swampy Stories Explorer

    Get kids excited before you go — listen to a story about the park and imagine the adventure ahead.

    Nature connection: Adds story and anticipation to a real outdoor trip.

    Open Swampy Stories Explorer

  • Stop 03 · Wildlife Hunt

    Seek by iNaturalist

    Discover plants, bugs, and animals — turn your walk into a real-life scavenger hunt.

    Nature connection: Helps kids notice and identify living things around them.

    Open Seek

  • Stop 04 · Sound Safari

    Merlin Bird ID

    Listen and identify birds around you — like a real-life Pokédex for nature.

    Nature connection: Makes bird sounds part of the adventure.

    Open Merlin Bird ID

  • Stop 05 · Treasure Mission

    Geocaching

    Add a mission to your walk — search for hidden treasures along the way.

    Nature connection: Gives kids a goal while exploring parks and trails.

    Open Geocaching

  • Stop 06 · Stargazing Finale

    Google Sky Map

    End the day by exploring the night sky — discover stars and planets together.

    Nature connection: Extends the outdoor adventure into the evening.

    Open Google Sky Map
